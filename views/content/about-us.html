ABOUT SFA

Ab Jeetega India

It all begins with a belief. An unapologetic and determined belief that India as a nation has the potential to become a great sporting nation at the global stage.

We believe that its our time to rise, to grow and to cement our rightful respectful place on the top.

With a goal to mobilize India to realize its true potential, SFA has formulated a holistic integrated digital and on ground sporting ecosystem that enables identification, nurture and empowerment of athletes across all sports. The journey which has begun with the revolutionization of grass-root level sports endeavors to impact and empower all stakeholders of Indian sport. SFA has developed the largest, high impact, level playing platform that gives young athletes, between the age of 6-18 years across 27 disciplines of sport (19 Olympic), an opportunity to showcase their talent and realize their potential through its annual SFA Olympic style CHAMPIONSHIPS.  SFA’s online portal (www.sfanow.in) brings together all sporting stakeholders from across all levels from institutions, federations, talent, corporations, sports ambassadors to academies on a single platform. Thereby, seamlessly connecting talent and opportunity through a single click.

SFA has been awarded the prestigious Start Up Award 2018-19 for its work towards radically transforming the sporting experience at the grass root level and for building a platform which will churn out the future champions for India.
